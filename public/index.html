About

An 8-sample pack is crafted by a producer or a community.

The challenge: two weeks to produce your track based on the 8 samples only, maximum length 3:20 minutes.

Tracks are released anonymously on our soundcloud account. During the first week, producers are encouraged to write feedbacks to their peers and pick a winner.

Winner of the round is announced and artists revealed after a 3-week cycle. Open to anyone interested and all styles welcome.
